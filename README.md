# Kerala Population and Education
This project analyses the population of Kerala with the Education trends across different districts 
It highlights key insights such as literacy rates, gender ratios, and urbanâ€“rural educational differences

## Dashboard
This Dashboard Visualizes
. Population distribution across districts
. Literates,Illiterates comparison
. Urban vs. Rural literacy differences
. Gender wise statistics of graduation
. Higher education trends 

## Insights
. *Malappuram* has the *highest population* and *highest literacy rate*.
. *Wayanad* records the *lowest literacy rate*.
. In *Rural* area *Malappuram* has the *highest number of Literates* while *Wayanad* has the *Fewest*.
. *Pathanamthitta,Idukki* and *Wayanad* have the *Highest Rural Area percentage*
. *Illiteracy* is more prevalent in *Rural Areas*
. In *Urban Population* and *Urban Literacy* *Eranakulam* Leads
. *Urban Areas* holds more *Female Graduates* than Rural
. *Diploma Programs* have *more males* especially in *Technical Diplomas*
. Mostly the *Interest in Education* remains in the *age group of 18-24*
